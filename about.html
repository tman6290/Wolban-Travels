Our Story

Born From a Love of Discovery

Wolban Travels Limited (RC 740352) was registered in April 2008 and commenced operations in May 2009 with one clear mission: to deliver world-class travel experiences to every client, every time.

What began as a passionate team of experienced travellers has grown into one of Nigeria’s most trusted independent travel agencies, proudly headquartered in Ikeja, Lagos. We’ve helped thousands of families, corporate clients, groups, and honeymooners explore destinations worldwide.

We specialise in business & group travel, vacation packages, corporate travel needs, hotel bookings, honeymoon packages and incentive travel. Our team are experienced travellers and industry professionals who bring genuine first-hand knowledge to every itinerary they craft for you.

  • Fully independent — not tied to any airline, hotel chain, or tour operator
  • NANTA accredited and IATA affiliated — your bookings are trusted, safe and professionally managed
  • Every destination we sell has been personally inspected by our team
  • Trusted by thousands of satisfied travellers since we commenced operations in 2009
