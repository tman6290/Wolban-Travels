Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies on our website

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies Wolban Travels Limited uses on this website (wolbantravels.com), and how you can manage your cookie preferences. Please read this policy alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owner. Cookies cannot carry viruses or install malware on your device.

There are several types of cookies:

  • Session cookies — temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies — cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
  • First-party cookies — set directly by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party cookies — set by a third-party service embedded in the website (e.g. a map provider).

2. Cookies We Use

Our website currently uses strictly necessary session cookies only. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. The cookies we set are essential for the website to function correctly and cannot be switched off without affecting how the site performs.

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
wolban_session Strictly Necessary Maintains your session state while you browse the website (e.g. remembers form progress). Session (deleted when browser is closed)
wolban_pref Functionality Stores lightweight user interface preferences (e.g. wishlist selections) to improve your browsing experience. 30 days

3. Cookies We Do Not Use

We do not currently use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics, Hotjar) — we do not track user behaviour for analysis purposes.
  • Advertising or targeting cookies — we do not display interest-based advertisements and do not share browsing data with ad networks.
  • Social media cookies — our social media icons are simple links and do not embed tracking scripts from social platforms.

4. Third-Party Services

Some pages on our website load resources from trusted third-party providers. These providers may set their own cookies independently of us. They include:

  • Google Fonts — used to load our website fonts (Playfair Display and Inter). Google may log that these resources were requested. Please see Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Font Awesome (Cloudflare CDN) — used to load icons displayed throughout the site. Cloudflare may collect standard server log information. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Unsplash — destination and package imagery is served from Unsplash's CDN. Unsplash may log image requests. See Unsplash's Privacy Policy.

We have no control over third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers if you have concerns.

5. How to Manage and Delete Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

Instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Apple Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data.

You can also opt out of certain third-party cookies by visiting the Your Online Choices website.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed. Changes take effect as soon as they are posted.
