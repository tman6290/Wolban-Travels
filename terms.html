1. Definitions

  • "We / Us / Wolban Travels" means Wolban Travels Limited, RC 740352.
  • "You / The Client" means the lead passenger and all persons named on the booking.
  • "Booking" means any confirmed travel arrangement made through Wolban Travels.
  • "Package" means any combination of two or more travel components (e.g. flights + hotel) sold by us as a single arrangement.
  • "Supplier" means any third-party provider (airline, hotel, transfer company, tour operator, etc.) whose services form part of your booking.

2. Making a Booking

All bookings begin with an enquiry. Once you have agreed to a quote and wish to proceed:

  1. You will be asked to confirm all passenger details in writing.
  2. A deposit invoice will be issued (see Section 3).
  3. Your booking is confirmed only upon receipt of your deposit and our written confirmation to you.

The lead passenger must be at least 18 years of age and accepts these terms on behalf of all persons named in the booking.

3. Deposits & Full Payment

Deposits: A non-refundable deposit of between 10% and 25% of the total booking value is required to confirm your booking. The exact deposit amount will be stated on your quote.

Balance payment: The full balance is due no later than 10 weeks (70 days) before your departure date, unless otherwise agreed in writing. For bookings made within 10 weeks of departure, full payment is required at the time of booking.

Late payment: Failure to pay the balance by the due date may result in your booking being cancelled. In such cases, cancellation charges will apply as set out in Section 5.

All prices are quoted in Nigerian Naira (NGN) unless otherwise stated. Prices are confirmed at the time of booking and are subject to change before your booking is confirmed.

4. Amendments

If you wish to change any aspect of a confirmed booking, please contact us as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate any changes, subject to availability. An administration fee may apply, in addition to any charges imposed by suppliers.

Changes made within 8 weeks of departure may be treated as a cancellation and re-booking, and cancellation charges may apply to the original booking.

5. Cancellations by You

All cancellations must be submitted to us in writing (email is acceptable). Cancellation charges are calculated from the date we receive your written notification:

Notice Period Before Departure Cancellation Charge
More than 70 days Deposit only (non-refundable)
43–70 days 50% of total booking cost
29–42 days 75% of total booking cost
15–28 days 90% of total booking cost
14 days or fewer 100% of total booking cost

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance that includes cancellation cover (see Section 8).

6. Cancellations or Changes by Us

We reserve the right to cancel or make significant changes to your booking in exceptional circumstances (e.g. natural disasters, civil unrest, supplier insolvency, or government travel advisories). In such cases, we will:

  • Inform you as soon as reasonably practicable
  • Offer a suitable alternative of equivalent or higher value, or a lower-cost alternative with a refund of the difference
  • Or offer a full refund of all monies paid to us, where no suitable alternative exists

7. Passports, Visas & Health Requirements

It is your sole responsibility to ensure that you and all travellers hold valid travel documents for your destination(s), including:

  • A valid passport (most destinations require at least 6 months' validity beyond your return date)

We can offer general guidance, but we accept no liability for any passenger being denied boarding or entry due to insufficient or incorrect documentation.

8. Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend that all clients take out comprehensive travel insurance covering cancellation, medical emergencies, repatriation, loss of luggage, and travel delay. We are happy to recommend insurance providers if required.

Wolban Travels accepts no liability for any loss, damage, or additional costs arising from your failure to hold adequate travel insurance.
