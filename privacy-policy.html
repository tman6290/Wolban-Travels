Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026. Wolban Travels Limited (RC 740352) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only when you voluntarily provide it to us. This may include:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number
  • Travel preferences: destination, travel dates, number of travellers, budget range, holiday type
  • Booking information: passport details, dietary requirements, and other information necessary to fulfil your booking (collected at the point of booking, not via the website enquiry form)
  • Communications: any messages or additional details you share with us by email, WhatsApp, or phone
  • Usage data: general, non-identifiable browsing data collected by your browser (see our Cookie Policy)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your holiday enquiries and prepare personalised itineraries
  • Process and manage your travel bookings
  • Communicate with you about your booking, including pre-departure information
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g. airline and immigration requirements)
  • Send you relevant travel offers and updates, where you have given consent to receive these

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, and we will never use it for purposes incompatible with those stated above.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the following legal bases under the NDPR 2019:

  • Consent: where you have ticked the consent box on our enquiry form or opted in to marketing communications
  • Contract performance: where processing is necessary to fulfil your travel booking
  • Legitimate interests: where we have a genuine business interest in processing your data that is not overridden by your rights
  • Legal obligation: where we are required to process data to comply with applicable laws

5. Sharing Your Data

To deliver your holiday, we may share your relevant data with:

  • Airlines and carriers — for ticket issuance and check-in purposes
  • Hotels and accommodation providers — for your reservation
  • Ground transportation and transfer providers
  • Tour operators and activity suppliers
  • IATA-affiliated systems — for global airline reservation management

All third parties we work with are required to handle your data securely and only for the specific purpose of fulfilling your booking. We do not permit them to use your data for their own marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Enquiry data (no booking made): up to 12 months from the date of enquiry
  • Booking data: up to 6 years from the date of travel, to comply with legal and financial obligations
  • Marketing consent records: until you withdraw consent

After these periods, your data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights

Under the NDPR 2019, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — request deletion of your data where there is no legitimate reason for us to continue processing it
  • Restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@wolbantravels.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. If you have concerns about how you are sharing data with us, please contact us directly by phone or in person.

9. Cookies

Our website uses only strictly necessary session cookies. We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies. Please see our Cookie Policy for full details.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
